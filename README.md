# Notoria

**Your beautiful, private space for notes and tasks — works fully offline, lives on your device.**

Visit: [notoria.lovable.app](https://notoria.lovable.app)

---

## ✨ What is Notoria?

Notoria is a calm, distraction-free productivity companion built for thinkers, writers, students, and doers. It blends a rich notes app with a powerful task board, all wrapped in an elegant interface that respects your time and your privacy.

No accounts. No syncing. No data leaving your device — unless you choose otherwise.

---

## 🌿 Highlights

### 📝 Notes that feel like paper, but smarter
- Rich text editor with images, code blocks, PDFs, and more
- **Reading mode by default** — double-tap to edit
- Auto-saves while you write, never loses a thought
- Pin, star, color-code, and organize notes your way
- Long-press for quick actions on any note
- 30-day trash with easy restore

### 🗂 Workspaces & Subcategories
- Group notes into custom workspaces with your own colors and icons
- Add subcategories for finer organization
- Drag, reorder, and personalize freely

### ✅ Tasks Kanban Board
- Visual To Do / In Progress / Done columns
- Long-term **projects** with progress tracking
- **Modules** to group related tasks within a project
- Recurring tasks that leave a "trail record" for every completed cycle
- Due-date reminders so nothing slips through

### 📄 Built-in PDF Viewer
- Open and read PDFs right inside your notes
- Save PDFs offline (up to 100MB) for reading anywhere
- Extract text from PDFs into editable notes

### 🎨 Themes that match your mood
- **Light** — clean and bright
- **Dark** — charcoal with warm gold accents
- **Purple Gradient** — a softer, more playful look
- Choose your font (Cambria, Times, Calibri, Georgia) and font size

### ☕ Notoria Coffee
If Notoria makes your life a little easier, you can support its development with a coffee — directly from the sidebar.

---

## 🔒 Private by design

Everything you write stays on your device using your browser's secure local storage. There's no cloud, no tracking, and no account required. Your notes are yours — period.

---

## 📱 Install it like an app

Notoria is a Progressive Web App. Install it on your phone, tablet, or desktop for a full app-like experience that works completely offline.

- **Mobile**: Tap the share/menu button in your browser → "Add to Home Screen"
- **Desktop**: Click the install icon in your browser's address bar
